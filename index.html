Biography

Hi, I'm Yulia!

Documentary film director, publicist and writer. In 1996 I had joine All-Russia State Television and Radio Broadcasting Company as a reporter, screenwriter and director of documentaries, special reports, and commercials.
In 2000 I released my documentary about Yuri Lyubimov, Russian stage director associated with the internationally renowned Taganka Theatre. After 2001 worked as the editor-in-chief of the documentary format on Culture TV Channel (Телеканал "Культура"), Channel One (Первый канал). Also I had directing Docudrama films.
In 2004-2005 studied Feature films and dramaturgy at the Higher Courses of Scriptwriters and Directors in Moscow. In 2006 my film “The Abduction of Europe” won the New York International Independent Film and Video Festival in the Best Documentary nomination. In RIA Novosti (РИА Новости), I developed documentary films for the Internet, as well as web documentaries, which were awarded with international prizes. In 2014, the documentary film "Degen" was nominated for the National Prize in the category of non-fiction film and television, the Laurel Branch (Prize) “LAVR”.
My lectures on cinema and multimedia video at the Higher School of Economics and other institutions becomes popular, and today available online. In 2011 the short fiction film “The One” written and directed by me, featuring famous Russian actress Alisa Grebenshchikova, became the winner in the short film competition at the Shanghai Film Festival.

Today I'm continuing to make new films, to raise up new cinema projects, lectures and courses.
